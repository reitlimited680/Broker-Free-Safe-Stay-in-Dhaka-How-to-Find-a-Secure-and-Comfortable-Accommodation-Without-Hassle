## How to Find a Safe Stay Without a Broker

If you want to avoid broker-related complications, consider the following steps:

1. Book Directly Through Trusted Service Apartment Providers  
   Many service apartment operators now offer direct booking options through their websites, social media pages, or customer service teams. Direct communication allows you to receive accurate information and transparent pricing.

2. Check Reviews and Ratings  
   Before confirming your stay, review feedback from previous guests. Genuine reviews can provide valuable insights into:

   - Cleanliness
   - Security
   - Customer service
   - Property condition
   - Overall guest experience

3. Verify Available Amenities  
   Always confirm what is included in the package. Some properties may advertise furnished accommodation but exclude essential services. Ask about:

   - Internet availability
   - Housekeeping schedules
   - Utility costs
   - Parking facilities
   - Security arrangements

4. Request Recent Photos or Virtual Tours  
   Current photos help ensure that the property matches its description. A virtual tour can provide an even clearer understanding of the apartment layout and condition.

5. Understand the Pricing Structure  
   Transparent pricing is one of the biggest advantages of direct booking. Ensure that you understand:

- Monthly or daily rental rates
- Security deposits
- Utility charges
- Additional service fees

Clear pricing helps prevent unexpected expenses later.
